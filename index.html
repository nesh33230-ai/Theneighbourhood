Post what you need, or what you can give — a ride, a spare hour, a ladder, a listening ear. Neighbors nearby will see your note and reach out.

Why a board, not an app

No accounts, no algorithm deciding who you meet. Just short, honest notes — pinned in the open, for anyone in the neighborhood to read and answer.

Before you post: this board is shared — anything you pin or reply with is visible to everyone who opens this page. Skip phone numbers, exact addresses, or other private details in your note; swap those once you've connected with someone you trust.
